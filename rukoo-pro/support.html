Frequently asked questions

Prayer times seem wrong. Ensure location is enabled and check Settings → Calculation Method. Try another method for your region.

Qibla compass not moving. The compass uses your device’s magnetometer. Avoid strong magnets or metal and ensure the app has required permissions.

Find Mosques opens Maps. The app uses your device’s maps app to search; results depend on the maps provider.
